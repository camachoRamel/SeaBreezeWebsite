About Sea Breeze

OUR BUSINESS

Sea Breeze is esteemed for its delectable cuisine, which is expertly prepared by our team. Our signature recipe have been perfected for years in the business. Along with the freshest seafood available in local markets across the province, we also have a wide selection of newly harvested ingredients to choose from. It's ideal for dining with friends, family, clients, and business partners because of the vibrant yet casual ambiance.


We have been offering a variety of delicious delicacies since 2001, the year it was established and is expertly situated close to the Pier of Virac. The business had a successful run up until 2019 when we relocated and rebranded into Dungeon by Sea Breeze at the basement of Shopbox Bldg San Jose, Virac, Catanduanes. Following the end of pandemic, Sea Breeze made its long-awaited comeback with a newly renovated building operating back home near its old site. The new Sea Breeze started operating on May 2023 where all the magic starts.


Come dine with us & experience an authentic #SignatureSeaBreeze meal. We look forward to serving you!
